An Ocean State Wave of Reading

Charting Rhode Island Center for the Book's Growth Since 2020

by Laura Romig

In 2020, the Rhode Island Center for the Book incoroporated as a nationwide nonprofit. Since then, the Center has expanded across the state, primarily through its two flagship programs, Reading Across Rhode Island and Kids Reading Across Rhode Island. Led by current director Kate Lentz, the Center's mission is to expand literacy across the state. RICFB is the Rhode Island branch of the National Center for the Book, which is housed under the Library of Congress.

About this project

I completed this project in partnership with the Brown University Swearer Center for Public Service and their CEDEC (Community-Engaged Data & Evaluation Collaborative) initiative, which seeks to partner Brown students with local organizations in order to meet their data needs. The RI Center for the Book provided their own partnership records as the dataset, which I supplemented with existing RI datasets as cited in the individual charts.
